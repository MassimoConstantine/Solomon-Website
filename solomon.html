Most AI optimizes for fluency. Solomon optimizes for truth. Every output carries a provenance chain, every claim is verifiable, and every rule is enforced at the structural level — not as a suggestion, but as a physical constraint the system cannot bypass.

02 — Privacy & Security

Structurally private.
Not a policy — a physics.

Promises break. Architecture doesn't. Your data isolation is enforced at the database, network, and deployment level. There is no setting to turn it off.

Complete isolation

Separate database, separate deployment, separate encryption keys. Your intelligence cannot leak to another client. It is structurally impossible.

No model training

Your data is never used to train, fine-tune, or improve any model. What you share stays exclusively yours. Period.

Full audit trail

European infrastructure

03 — How Truth Gets Made

Every claim earns its place.

Solomon doesn't generate and hope. It fetches evidence, validates against verified sources, computes confidence — then delivers. If validation fails at any step, the output is rejected. Not flagged. Rejected.

04 — Truth Protocol

Five axioms. Absolute.
They override everything.

Reality outranks coherence.
External evidence wins over internal narrative. A beautiful story that contradicts data is wrong. Always.
Provenance is mandatory.
Every output carries its source, timestamp, transformation steps, and confidence. No provenance — output rejected.
Recursion must pay rent.
Every additional pass must prove measurable improvement. Thinking harder is not free. Default is one pass.
Write ≠ Believe.
The system's own output is suspect until verified through promotion gates. Generation is never belief.
Separation of concerns.
Ideation, retrieval, reasoning, and action each have separate permissions. No unbroken chain from hallucination to execution.
05 — Enforcement

Rules that cannot be broken.
Not rules that ask nicely.

Solomon's governance isn't an instruction layer that AI may follow. It's structural — enforced at the database, schema, and runtime level. The system physically cannot violate its own rules.
